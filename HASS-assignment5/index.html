Assignment 5 - Project Proposal

Sypnosis:

Social network analysis has made significant strides in visualization techniques over the years. However, there are still some areas that could benefit from further development. This project aims to develop a dashboard to provide data visualization on social network data. The dashboard will be focusing on dynamic visualization, user interactivity and engagement and data streaming from multiple social media platform such as twitter X, reddit, facebook, instagram and etc.

The primary data will be coming from twitter X. Data streaming from Reddit, Facebook and Instagram will be considered at the later stage.

Analyzing social media data provides the insights on trends and patterns in user behavior, interests, and preferences. By monitoring hashtags, keywords, and topics that are trending, businesses can stay updated on popular conversations and adapt their strategies accordingly.

Sentiment analysis techniques can be applied to social media data to determine the overall sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral) associated with a particular topic, brand, or event. This insight can help businesses gauge public opinion, monitor brand reputation, and identify potential PR crises.

Analyzing social media engagement metrics such as likes, shares, comments, and click-through rates can help businesses understand which types of content resonate best with their audience and provide valuable insights into customer satisfaction levels, pain points, and areas for improvement. This insight can inform content creation strategies and optimize content for maximum impact.
